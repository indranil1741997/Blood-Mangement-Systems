Blood donation is an integral part of the health and well being of the community.The need is constant, and there is no substitute for volunteer blood donations. Every 2 seconds, someone needs a blood transfusion.And, each donation can help save up to three lives.

Advantages

-Improves overall cardiovascular health
-Reduce the chance of heart attacks
-Lowers the risk of stroke
-Enhances the production of new blood cells
-Lowers risk of cancer
